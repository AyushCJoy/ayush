Ayush Cheruvallathu Joy As a User Experience and User Interface Designer, I am passionate about using keen observation and research to solve real-world design problems. With a natural creativity and an eye for aesthetics, I strive to make human interactions easier and more enjoyable through intuitive design.

My proficiency in design software such as Photoshop, Illustrator, Sketch, InDesign, and After Effects, as well as web development languages such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, allows me to create visually appealing and functional designs that enhance the user experience. Furthermore, my ability to utilize emerging technologies such as AI, including chat GPT and other emerging AI tools aid my understanding of topics and allows me to incorporate innovative solutions into my design work.

In addition to my design experience, I have also worked as a UX designer at Inago and X Sensa Labs, where I honed my skills in design research, wireframing, prototyping, and usability testing. As a Warehouse Supervisor at Canadian Tire, I also gained valuable experience implementing inventory sorting systems, which required a deep understanding of user needs and efficient design principles. Overall, my diverse skillset and experience in UX/UI design and product development make me a versatile and adaptable designer who can take on a variety of design challenges with confidence and creativity.
